Privacy Policy

1. We may be running ads supplied by Google Adsense. In the European Economic Area (EEA), AdSense uses a cookie to ensure that it does not show you personalized ads. Outside the EEA, Google Adsense uses cookies to show you personalized ads.

2. We also may use Google Analytics. This enables us to monitor and analyze web traffic so that we can improve the website and user experience. Google Analytics collects anonymous user data and stores it for a limited time per their privacy policy. If you’re uncomfortable with Google Analytics, you might consider a Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.



If you aren’t fine with our use of cookies you can either disable them or avoid using our website.
